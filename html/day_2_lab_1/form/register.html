Difference between get and post

get: when you click on the button, the data will be sent to the server and the page will be refreshed. The data will not be saved.
get: also when get data from the server
post: when you click on the button, the data will be sent to the server and the page will not be refreshed. The data will be saved.
post: also when you submit the form
